About us

White, greasy hair almost fully covers a craggy, friendly face. Shuttered hazel eyes, set a-symmetrically within their sockets, watch devotedly over the families they've rarely felt at home at for so long. A goatee handsomely compliments his mouth and leaves a bittersweet memory of his fortunate looks. This is the face of Irving Faley, a true idealist among werewolves. He stands seductively among others, despite his lean frame. There's something irregular about him, perhaps it's his bravery or perhaps it's simply his warmth. But nonetheless, people tend to lie about knowing him to brag, while commending him for his deeds.
